## Featured Projects

### 🧬 [DrugMind](https://mokangmedical.github.io/DrugMind/)
AI-powered drug discovery platform — from molecular design to lead optimization.

### 📊 [PharmaSim](https://mokangmedical.github.io/PharmaSim/)
Pharmaceutical market simulation with 7-dimensional expert evaluation.

### 🏥 [MediChat-RD](https://mokangmedical.github.io/medichat-rd/)
Rare disease AI diagnosis platform with knowledge graph and drug repurposing.

### 🎤 [VoiceHealth](https://mokangmedical.github.io/voicehealth/)
AI voice health detection — analyze voice biomarkers in 30 seconds.

### 📈 [Tianyan](https://mokangmedical.github.io/tianyan/)
Business intelligence and commercial prediction platform.

- **Data**: PostgreSQL, Redis, Elasticsearch
