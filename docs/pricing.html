Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our pricing and plans

Is the Free tier really free forever?

Yes! All our open-source projects are released under MIT or Apache 2.0 licenses. You can use, modify, and deploy them at no cost. The Free tier gives you full access to the source code and community support.

Can I switch between plans?

Absolutely. You can upgrade from Free to Pro at any time. If you need to move to Enterprise, our sales team will help you transition seamlessly. Downgrading takes effect at the end of your billing cycle.

What does "custom model fine-tuning" include?

Pro users can fine-tune our AI models on their own datasets through our managed pipeline. This includes training infrastructure, model versioning, and evaluation dashboards. Enterprise users get additional customization options.

Do you offer academic discounts?

Yes! We offer significant discounts for academic institutions and research organizations. Contact us at contact@opc-homepage.com with your institution details for special pricing.

What compliance standards do you support?

Enterprise tier includes support for HIPAA, GDPR, and other regional healthcare data regulations. We work with your compliance team to ensure all deployments meet your regulatory requirements.
